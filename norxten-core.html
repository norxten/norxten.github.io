Who it's for

Built for people who manage projects in the field

General Contractors

Run multiple job sites with full visibility on crew hours, material costs, and project budgets — all from your phone.

Site Supervisors

Clock in your team, review the day's work, and submit receipts without leaving the site or touching a laptop.

Project Managers

Get clean reports on labour, expenses, and budget status for every project. No spreadsheets, no guesswork.

Capabilities

Everything connected, nothing missed

Crew Management

Build your roster, assign workers to projects, and keep employee records in one place. Add or remove active workers by the month.

GPS Work Session Validation

Clock-ins and clock-outs are tied to GPS location. Verify that work sessions happened on-site — not from a parking lot or home.

Receipt & Invoice Capture

Point your camera at any receipt or invoice. OCR reads the amount, vendor, and date automatically — no typing required.

OCR-Based Cost Tracking

Every scanned document feeds directly into your project budget. Track spending against estimates in real time.

Project Reporting

Labour hours, total costs, budget variance — clear dashboards for every active project. Pro plan includes PDF export and finished project archive.

Mobile-First Design

Built for iOS and Android. Fast, offline-tolerant, and designed for use with gloves on and sun in your eyes.

How it works

From job site to report in four steps

01

Set up your project and crew

Create a project, set a budget, and add your workers. Takes less than five minutes.

02

Workers clock in at the site

GPS confirms they're on location. Hours are logged automatically — no paper timesheets.

03

Snap receipts and invoices as they arrive

OCR captures the details. Every cost is assigned to the right project instantly.

04

Review your project dashboard

See labour hours, total spend, and budget status at a glance. Export a PDF report when the project closes (Pro plan).
