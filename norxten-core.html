Core capabilities

Everything in one app.

No spreadsheets. No paper. No guessing.

📸

AI Receipt & Invoice Scanning

Point your camera at any receipt or invoice. OCR and AI extract vendor, amounts, and taxes automatically. No manual entry.

👷

Crew Management

Invite employees, assign to projects, manage your team. QR code punch-in/out with GPS verification at every session.

⏱

Labour & Overtime Tracking

Automatic OT calculation per employee, per day. Real labour cost per project — updated in real time.

📈

Job Cost Tracking

See total job cost — materials, labour, overhead — updated the moment a receipt is scanned or session ends.

💳

Budget vs Actual

Set initial budgets. Record formal revisions separately. Get alerts before you go over budget.

📋

Project Closure Reports

PDF and CSV at project finish — full cost breakdown, profit margin, labour summary — ready to share.

📍

GPS Work Validation

GPS coordinates captured at punch-in and punch-out verify on-site presence. Flag and review exceptions.

🧾

GST/QST Handling

Built-in support for Canadian tax labels. GST and QST extracted and tracked separately per receipt.

🔔

Smart Notifications

Budget overrun alerts, schedule warnings, and crew punch-in/out events — all configurable.
