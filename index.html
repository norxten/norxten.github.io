What's an active employee?

Anyone who clocks in at least once during the billing month. Add or remove workers anytime — you're only billed for who actually works.

Can I switch plans?

Yes, upgrade or downgrade anytime. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle. No penalties.

What's included in the free trial?

About Norxten

Built in Quebec.
Built for the field.

Norxten Technologies Inc. is a software company focused entirely on the construction and contracting industry. We build tools that help general contractors run tighter operations — less guesswork, more control.

The Norxten Core app is our first product. It was designed around one question: what does a GC actually need to know, right now, on a job site? The answer is crew hours, real costs, and budget status — without opening a spreadsheet.

We're based in Quebec, Canada and operate in both English and French.
