Our product

The Norxten Core app

Built for general contractors who need to know — right now — what a project actually costs. Job expenses, crew hours, and project performance from your phone.

01

Job cost tracking

See total job cost — materials, labour, overhead — updated in real time the moment a receipt is scanned or a session ends.

02

Crew cost visibility

Labour cost per project, per employee — with automatic overtime calculation and GPS-verified punch-in/out.

03

Receipt & invoice capture

AI-powered OCR reads vendor, amounts, and taxes from any receipt or invoice. No manual data entry.

04

Missing invoice alerts

Flag unverified expenses before they become budget surprises. Every receipt is accounted for, every tax captured.

05

Crew management & GPS validation

QR code punch-in with GPS verification. Know who is actually on site and when. Flag suspicious sessions for review.

06

Project closure reports

PDF and CSV at project finish — full cost breakdown, profit margin, labour summary — ready to send to your client.

Capabilities

Everything in one app.

No spreadsheets. No paper. No guessing.

AI receipt & invoice scanning

Point your camera at any receipt or invoice. OCR and AI extract vendor, amounts, and taxes automatically.

Crew management

Invite employees, assign to projects, manage your entire team. QR punch-in/out with GPS verification.

Labour & overtime tracking

Automatic OT calculation per employee, per day. Real labour cost per project — updated in real time.

Budget vs actual

Set initial budgets, record formal revisions separately. Get alerts before you go over budget.

Project closure reports

PDF and CSV reports at project finish — full cost breakdown, labour summary, profit margin.

GPS work validation

GPS coordinates captured at punch-in and punch-out verify on-site presence. Flag and review exceptions.
