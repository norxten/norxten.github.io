About Norxten Technologies Inc.

We build software for people who build things.

Norxten Technologies Inc. is a federally incorporated Canadian company based in Quebec.

Who we are

Focused on the field.

Norxten Technologies Inc. builds practical software for general contractors and field-based businesses. We focus on operational clarity, cost control, and reliable project data — helping companies run crews, track expenses, and manage projects with greater confidence.

Our goal is to create tools that support real work in the field, not just office reporting. That means software designed around how contractors actually operate: moving between job sites, managing teams, capturing receipts and invoices, tracking labour, and keeping project costs visible at all times.

Our first product, the Norxten Core app, is a mobile platform built to help contractors manage crew activity, receipt and invoice capture, labour tracking, and project reporting from the field.
