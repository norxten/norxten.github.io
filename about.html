Who we are

A focused software company rooted in the field.

Norxten Technologies Inc. is a Quebec-based software company. We build platforms for contractors, field operations teams, and anyone who manages crews, receipts, and project budgets in the real world.

Our first product, Norxten Core, is a mobile-first app that handles crew scheduling, GPS-validated timekeeping, OCR-based cost capture, and project reporting — everything a general contractor needs in one place.

We believe that the best software for the trades is software that disappears into the workflow — fast, reliable, and built for people who don't sit at a desk.
