Privacy Policy

Norxten Core — Norxten Technologies Inc.

EN — English FR — Français
Last Updated: April 23, 2026    Effective Date: April 23, 2026

1. Who We Are

Norxten Technologies Inc. (hereinafter, “Norxten”) is a federally incorporated Canadian company with an office in Quebec, Canada, and registered in Quebec.

Norxten Core is the mobile application, related web pages, backend services, and associated platform features operated by Norxten for general contractors and similar business users.

This Privacy Policy explains how Norxten collects, uses, discloses, stores, protects, and otherwise processes personal information in connection with Norxten Core.

For privacy questions, requests, or complaints, contact: Privacy Officer — privacy@norxten.com

2. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to personal information collected:

  • when account owners, employees, and other users access or use Norxten Core
  • when Customer organizations create or administer accounts
  • when users upload receipts, invoices, project data, labour/session data, or related records
  • when users contact support or interact with Norxten Core
  • through service providers used to operate Norxten Core

This Privacy Policy does not apply to third-party websites, app stores, payment platforms, or third-party services that have their own privacy notices.

3. Business Service Context

Norxten Core is a business software product. In many cases, the organization that subscribes to and administers Norxten Core determines what employee, contractor, project, receipt, and location data is entered into the platform.

  • the subscribing company or organization (the “Customer”) is responsible for ensuring it has the legal authority to collect and provide the relevant data and to use Norxten Core in compliance with applicable law, workplace obligations, and internal policies
  • Norxten processes personal information in order to provide, secure, support, maintain, and improve Norxten Core

4. Personal Information We Collect

A. Account and Identity Information

  • name, email address, phone number, company name, account role, profile details

B. Project and Financial Information

  • project names, addresses, budgets, dates, cost and labour records
  • receipt and invoice images, OCR-extracted text and structured fields
  • vendor names, dates, subtotals, totals, tax data, descriptions

C. Workforce and Session Information

  • employee invitation and account administration data
  • work session, travel session, and time records
  • punch-in, punch-out, travel, and arrival event records

D. Location Information

  • GPS/location data collected at specific workflow events such as punch-in, punch-out, travel start, and arrival confirmations, where enabled and permitted on the device

E. Technical and Device Information

  • device type, operating system, app version, push notification token, authentication/session information, error logs, crash information, diagnostic information

F. Support and Communication Information

  • support requests, feedback, communications with Norxten, troubleshooting details and attachments

5. How We Use Personal Information

Norxten may use personal information to:

  • provide, operate, and maintain Norxten Core
  • authenticate users and manage accounts, memberships, and subscriptions
  • process receipts and invoices, including OCR and related parsing/extraction workflows
  • calculate labour costs, overtime, budgets, schedules, and project financials
  • generate project reports, exports, archives, and administrative records
  • manage project, crew, and session workflows
  • send push notifications, alerts, and account/service communications
  • troubleshoot issues, monitor performance, detect abuse, and improve security
  • develop, test, improve, and support Norxten Core
  • comply with law, enforce agreements, and protect rights, property, and safety

6. Location Data

Norxten Core may collect precise location data at specific work-related events, such as punch-in, punch-out, travel start, and arrival confirmations, in order to validate the integrity of the related work session or attendance record.

Based on the current product design, Norxten Core is not intended to continuously track a user’s location in the background.

If Norxten Core is used by an employer or another organization, that organization is responsible for ensuring any required workplace notices, permissions, consents, or authorizations are provided under applicable law.

7. Plans, Roles, and Product Features

Norxten Core supports two user roles: owner and employee. It offers plan-based access including Starter, Field, and Pro, as well as trial and grace access periods.

8. When We Share Personal Information

A. Within the Customer Organization

To authorized users within the same Customer organization, subject to the role and permissions structure of Norxten Core.

B. To Service Providers

To vendors and service providers that help operate Norxten Core, including providers of cloud hosting and storage, database infrastructure, OCR or AI-assisted document processing, push notification delivery, subscription and billing management, app store purchase processing, analytics, monitoring, diagnostics, and support tools. These may include Supabase, OCR/AI processing providers, RevenueCat, Apple, Google, and Firebase.

C. For Legal, Security, and Safety Reasons

Where required or permitted by law, including to comply with legal obligations, court orders, or lawful requests, and to investigate suspected fraud, abuse, or security incidents.

D. Business Transactions

In connection with a merger, acquisition, financing, sale of assets, reorganization, or similar business transaction, subject to appropriate confidentiality and legal safeguards.

Norxten does not sell personal information.

9. Cross-Border Processing

Norxten and its service providers may process or store personal information in Canada, the United States, or other jurisdictions where Norxten or its service providers operate. Where required, Norxten uses contractual, organizational, and technical safeguards in connection with cross-border processing.

10. Safeguards

Norxten uses administrative, technical, contractual, and organizational safeguards designed to protect personal information against unauthorized access, disclosure, loss, misuse, or alteration. These may include encryption in transit, access controls, role-based restrictions, session/authentication controls, logging and monitoring, and limited internal access on a need-to-know basis. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

11. Retention

Norxten retains personal information for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, including to provide Norxten Core, maintain operational and legal records, and comply with legal obligations. Deletion requests remain subject to legal, backup, security, dispute-resolution, and operational retention exceptions.

12. Your Rights

Subject to applicable law and verification of identity or authority, you may have the right to:

  • request access to personal information held by Norxten
  • request correction of inaccurate personal information
  • withdraw consent where withdrawal is legally available
  • request deletion, subject to lawful exceptions
  • request a copy of your personal information in a structured, machine-readable format
  • file a complaint with an applicable privacy regulator

To exercise a privacy right, contact: privacy@norxten.com

13. Security Incidents

If Norxten becomes aware of a security incident involving personal information, Norxten may investigate, mitigate, document, and notify affected persons, Customers, regulators, law enforcement, or other parties as required or permitted by applicable law.

14. Children

Norxten Core is a business application and is not intended for children under 18.

15. Changes to This Privacy Policy

Norxten may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version and update the “Last Updated” date. Where required, we may provide additional notice.
