Privacy Policy

Norxten Technologies Inc. — Effective: April 22, 2026

Norxten Technologies Inc. ("Norxten", "we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of users of our products and website. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use Norxten Core or visit norxten.com.

By using our services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our services.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create a Norxten account, we collect your name, email address, and organization name. You may also provide a phone number and billing information.

Employee and Crew Data

If you use Norxten Core to manage workers, you may enter employee names, roles, and work schedules. This data is entered by you and is under your control.

Location Data

Norxten Core uses GPS to validate work sessions. Location data is collected only when a user actively clocks in or clocks out. We do not continuously track device location in the background.

Usage Data

We collect information about how you interact with our app and website, including pages visited, features used, device type, operating system, and app version. This data helps us improve the product.

Financial and Project Data

Cost data, receipts, invoice images, and project budgets that you enter into Norxten Core are stored securely and are accessible only to authorized users within your account.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the Norxten Core application
  • To validate work sessions using GPS coordinates
  • To process OCR on receipts and invoices you submit
  • To generate project reports and dashboards
  • To send transactional emails (account confirmations, billing receipts)
  • To respond to support requests
  • To improve our products through aggregated, anonymized analytics
  • To comply with applicable law

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure cloud infrastructure. We use industry-standard encryption for data in transit (TLS) and at rest. Access to production data is strictly limited to authorized personnel.

While we take reasonable precautions, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. If you close your account, we will delete your personal data within 90 days, except where we are required to retain it by law.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Service providers who assist us in operating our platform (e.g., cloud hosting, payment processing, analytics), under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal authorities if required by applicable law, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of Norxten or its users.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your province or country of residence, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@norxten.com or info@norxten.com.

Quebec residents: This policy is intended to comply with Quebec's Law 25 (Act respecting the protection of personal information in the private sector).

7. Children's Privacy

Norxten Core is designed for business use by adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under the age of 16. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by email or through a notice in the app. Continued use of our services after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
